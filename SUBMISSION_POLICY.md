## Final Submission Rules

- The last commit timestamp before the deadline will be considered final.
- No commits will be accepted after the deadline.
- All repositories will be archived immediately at deadline time.

---

## Repository Archiving

At the deadline:

- All repositories will be converted to read-only mode.
- No further commits, pull requests, or changes will be possible.
- Archived repositories will be used for judging.

---

## Important

Teams are advised to:

- Push changes regularly.
- Avoid last-minute submissions.
- Ensure the repository is in runnable condition before deadline.
