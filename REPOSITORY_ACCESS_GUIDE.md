## How You Will Receive Access

1. You will be added as a collaborator to your assigned repository.
2. You will receive an email notification from GitHub.
3. Accept the invitation immediately.

---

## Access Permissions

- Team Leaders will receive Admin access to their repository.
- Team Members will receive Write access.
- Reviewers will receive Read-only access.

---

## Important

- You cannot see other team repositories.
- Do not attempt to access repositories that are not assigned to you.
- Report access issues immediately.
